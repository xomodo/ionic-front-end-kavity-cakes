Our History

Started in 2010, Kavity Cakes quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in New Jersey, USA. With its unique brand of world class cakes that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele. Featuring our star chef from the French Culinary institute in New York, you never know what style of cakes will arrive the next time you visit us.

The venture traces its humble beginnings to The Philippines where the chef's parents are from.
